[session_4] Discover the price range customers are willing to pay and their price sensitivity.
[session_4] The goal is to understand the real meaning of "too expensive"

[session_4] - Do NOT ask price sensitivity before confirming value perception

[session_4]    - Price too expensive to purchase?
[session_4]    - Price expensive but worth considering?
[session_4]    - Price that feels appropriate?
[session_4]    - Price so cheap quality is suspect?
[session_4] 4. Pricing model preference: subscription vs one-time vs usage-based
[session_4] 5. Purchase decision triggers: under what conditions would they actually pay?

[session_4] ## Question Flow Order

[session_4] - Q1~2: Identify current related spending (anchoring)
[session_4] - Q3~4: Explore how they evaluate the value of this service
